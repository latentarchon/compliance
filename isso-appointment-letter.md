## Appointment

I, **Andrew Hendel**, Chief Executive Officer of Latent Archon, LLC, hereby formally appoint myself as the **Information System Security Officer (ISSO)** for the Latent Archon Document Intelligence Platform (LA-DIP), effective immediately.

## Responsibilities

As ISSO, I am responsible for:

1. **Security Posture Management** — Maintaining the overall security posture of the LA-DIP system, including monitoring, assessment, and continuous improvement of security controls.

2. **Compliance Oversight** — Ensuring the system operates in accordance with NIST SP 800-53 Rev. 5 (Moderate baseline), FedRAMP requirements, and all applicable federal security standards.

3. **Plan of Action & Milestones** — Managing the POA&M, tracking remediation of identified findings, and reporting progress to the Authorizing Official.

4. **Continuous Monitoring** — Overseeing the continuous monitoring program, including automated evidence collection, vulnerability scanning, red team exercises, and incident response readiness.

5. **Configuration Management** — Approving significant changes to the system and ensuring all changes follow the established change management process (POL-CM-001).

6. **Incident Response** — Serving as Incident Commander for security incidents and ensuring compliance with FedRAMP Incident Communications Procedures (POL-IR-001 §8.3).

7. **Authorization Package** — Maintaining the System Security Plan (SSP), OSCAL package, and all supporting documentation for FedRAMP authorization.

8. **Personnel Security** — Ensuring all personnel with system access have completed required security training, background checks (where applicable), and Rules of Behavior acceptance.

9. **Agency Liaison** — Serving as the primary security point of contact for the sponsoring agency, FedRAMP PMO, and any Third Party Assessment Organization (3PAO).

## Qualifications

- Direct operational control of all system components (infrastructure, application, data)
- Hands-on management of security controls implementation on GCP

## Duration

This appointment is effective until:
- A dedicated ISSO is hired and formally appointed, OR
- The system is decommissioned, OR
- This appointment is superseded by a new appointment letter
